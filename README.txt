Coming to cube-town, you set out on the only thing you do best: giving out free hugs!
Spread the love, whether they like it or not.


How to Play:
- WASD to move.
- Toggle music in top right corner via button.
- (while in air) Left mouse to toggle glide.
- (while stationary) Right mouse to toggle binocular vision.
- Gain score and time by giving your cubed neighbourhood a nice hug.
- You'll gradually gain speed the more love you give out.
- Find all your cubed buddies to win.
